About cardiovascular prediction model

The cardiovascular prediction model is a machine learning model trained on the Framingham Heart Study dataset. The model was trained on over 7000 rows of data, making it robust and reliable. It predicts the likelihood of cardiovascular disease based on user inputs such as age, gender, cholesterol levels, and lifestyle factors. This model helps in early detection and prevention of cardiovascular diseases.
